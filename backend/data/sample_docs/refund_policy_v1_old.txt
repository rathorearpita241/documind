REFUND POLICY v1.0 — Effective December 2023

Section 3: Bulk Order Refund Policy
For bulk orders of 100 units or more, customers are eligible for a 10-day return window with store credit only. Orders below 100 units follow the standard 7-day policy.

Section 3.1: Bulk Discount Threshold
Bulk pricing applies to orders of 100 units or more. The discount schedule is:
- 100-199 units: 10% discount
- 200+ units: 14% discount
