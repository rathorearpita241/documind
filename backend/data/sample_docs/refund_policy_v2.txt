REFUND POLICY v2.0 — Effective March 2024

Section 1: General Returns
All products purchased from our catalog are eligible for return within 7 days of delivery for individual orders (1-49 units). Returns must be in original packaging with proof of purchase.

Section 2: Damaged or Defective Goods
If items arrive damaged or defective, customers must report within 48 hours. A full replacement or refund will be issued within 5 business days of confirmation.

Section 3: Bulk Order Refund Policy
For bulk orders of 50 units or more, customers are eligible for a 15-day return window with full account credit. This supersedes the standard 7-day policy. Partial returns on bulk orders are accepted at a 10% restocking fee.

Section 3.2: Bulk Discount Threshold
Bulk pricing applies to orders of 50 units or more. The discount schedule is as follows:
- 50-99 units: 8% discount
- 100-199 units: 12% discount
- 200+ units: 16% discount

Section 4: International Orders
International customers (outside India) are subject to the same return windows. However, return shipping costs are borne by the customer. Credit is issued after inspection of returned goods.

Section 5: Non-Returnable Items
Custom-manufactured items, perishable goods, and items marked as clearance are not eligible for return unless defective.
